# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, track key metrics, and provide insights to support better business decisions.

It focuses on turning raw data into clear, visual reports that help identify trends, top performers, and opportunities for improvement.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Track revenue and sales performance over time
* Monitor key business metrics (KPIs)
* Identify top customers and products
* Analyze trends and patterns
* Improve reporting through interactive visuals

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 💰 Sales Performance

* Total revenue tracking
* Monthly and yearly trends
* Sales growth analysis
* Performance comparison over time

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Top customers by revenue
* Customer contribution analysis
* Behavior and activity patterns

### 📦 Product Analysis

* Top-performing products
* Revenue by category
* Product performance comparison

### 📊 KPI Tracking

* Total revenue
* Total sales quantity
* Customer metrics
* Trend indicators

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

* Cleaned raw data using Power Query
* Handled missing and inconsistent values
* Created relationships between tables
* Structured data model for efficient analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* A small percentage of customers generates a large portion of revenue
* Certain products consistently drive higher sales
* Sales trends show clear growth periods and slower periods
* Some categories underperform and may need attention

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

* Focus on retaining high-value customers
* Increase investment in top-performing products
* Review and improve underperforming categories
* Use trend data to support planning and forecasting

---

## 💼 Business Value

This dashboard helps:

* Make faster, data-driven decisions
* Understand sales and customer performance
* Reduce time spent on manual reporting
* Communicate insights clearly to stakeholders

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop
2. Use filters and slicers to explore the data
3. Navigate between pages to analyze different areas
